Research Opportunities

Atmospheric Science Research Laboratory

Weather and Climate Extremes Research Group

Join SRM-ATMOS

SRM-ATMOS welcomes motivated students, researchers and collaborators interested in atmospheric sciences, weather forecasting, climate modelling, lightning science, satellite meteorology and artificial intelligence applications.

Ph.D. Opportunities

Applications are invited from highly motivated candidates interested in pursuing doctoral research in atmospheric sciences and related interdisciplinary areas.

  • Weather and Climate Extremes
  • Numerical Weather Prediction (WRF)
  • Lightning Science and Atmospheric Electricity
  • Satellite Meteorology
  • Climate Modelling
  • Physics-Informed Artificial Intelligence
  • Hydrometeorology and Extreme Rainfall

Student Research Projects

B.Tech. Projects

Research projects involving weather data analysis, Python programming, AI applications and atmospheric observations.

M.Tech. Projects

Advanced studies using numerical weather prediction models, climate simulations and atmospheric datasets.

M.Sc. Projects

Research in atmospheric sciences, climate variability, lightning and satellite meteorology.

Internships

Short-term and long-term internships are available for students interested in atmospheric science research.

  • Weather Data Analysis
  • Python Programming for Atmospheric Sciences
  • Satellite Data Processing
  • WRF Modelling
  • Lightning Research
  • Artificial Intelligence Applications

Research Collaboration

SRM-ATMOS welcomes collaborative research proposals from universities, research institutions, government agencies and industry partners.

Areas of collaboration include:

  • Weather Forecasting
  • Climate Change and Climate Extremes
  • Atmospheric Observations
  • Lightning Science
  • Hydrometeorology
  • Artificial Intelligence for Earth System Science

Atmospheric Science Research Laboratory

Weather and Climate Extremes Research Group

Overview

SRM-ATMOS is actively engaged in research on weather and climate extremes, lightning science, atmospheric electricity, satellite meteorology, numerical weather prediction and artificial intelligence applications. The laboratory has contributed through publications, research supervision, scientific outreach and collaborative research activities.

Research Publications

  • Research publications in national and international peer-reviewed journals.
  • Contributions in atmospheric sciences, climate studies and weather forecasting.
  • Research outputs involving satellite meteorology, lightning science and numerical modelling.
  • Collaborative publications with national and international researchers.

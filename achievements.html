Major Research Contributions

  • Climate change projections using CMIP6 climate simulations.
  • Extreme rainfall assessment over India and Southern Peninsular India.
  • Lightning climatology and lightning hazard prediction studies.
  • Atmospheric electricity and electric field variability investigations.
  • Numerical weather prediction using the WRF modelling framework.
  • Application of Physics-Informed Artificial Intelligence for rainfall forecasting.
  • Satellite meteorology applications using INSAT, GPM and MODIS datasets.

Weather Monitoring and Forecasting

The SRM-ATMOS Weather Dashboard provides access to weather monitoring and forecasting resources relevant to atmospheric science research, teaching and outreach activities. The dashboard supports studies on weather and climate extremes, lightning activity, satellite meteorology and numerical weather prediction.
